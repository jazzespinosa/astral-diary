Our Mission

Astral Diary was crafted to be a serene space where your daily reflections live among the stars. We believe everyone deserves a private, beautiful sanctuary to chronicle their journey through the cosmos of life — free from noise, distraction, and prying eyes.

Features

  • Write entries in a clean, focused environment.
  • Navigate history with an astronomical calendar.
  • Personalize with celestial backgrounds.
  • Protected by end-to-end encryption.
  • Securely sign in with Google.
  • Recycle bin for recovered entries.
 End-to-End Encryption

Your secrets are safe in the cosmos.

Every diary entry you write is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves your browser. Data travels from client to server fully encrypted — no one can read your thoughts in transit.

/
Your Device
Server

The master key is securely held server-side. Your data is stored encrypted at rest, keeping it safe even if the database were ever compromised.
